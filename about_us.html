About Our Company

Leading the Way in Innovative Packaging Solutions

Mahavir Packaging and Rishaan print pack, is engaged in the manufacturing of outer and inner cartons (duplex and corrugated boxes ) for the past 20 years. We are centrally located in Nasik at Satpur MIDC. We have another plant in a prime location at Vapi , with a factory and office building area of 11000 sq. ft. Besides that we have 2 godowns in Chakan , where we stock the boxes that are manufactured in Nasik. We have skilled staff to operate our godowns there. In next 2 months we will be starting a auto plant at Chakan with 18000 sq feet area. At Nashik we have a big shop floor and an equally big office area, wherein we have a good trained staff and 30 people working on the shop floor including our supervisors , skilled operators and helpers. We also have a laboratory with good testing equipment for our quality control. Besides Nasik being an agrobase center, big Heidleberg printing machines are available with a few printers who also provide 8 color printing jobs. We are associated with all such printers here. We have a sister concern firm where we have a Paper Trading business. In Nashik, which helps us to procure paper at a lower rate , and hence we offer competitive rates. We are the leaders in box manufacturing.
